COMPONENT CREATION DESCRIPTION


CRICKET

The game is played with a bat and ball, with the batting team aiming to score as many runs as possible, and the bowling team trying to dismiss the batsmen.Cricket matches can take various formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and (T20) matches.The sport has a rich history.

FOOTBALL

Football is the world's most popular sport. It is played by two teams, each consisting of 11 players. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's net. The first set of rules established in England in the 19th century. The FIFA World Cup, held every four years.

KABADDI

Kabaddi is a contact team sport played between two teams of seven players, originating from India. The objective of the game is for a single player on offense, referred to as a "raider", to run into the opposing team's half of the court, all without being tackled by the defenders in 30 seconds.

CHESS

Chess is a two-player strategy board game that originated in India around the 6th century. The game is played on an 8x8 grid, and each player controls 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, knights, bishops, and pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king.

BADMITTON

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are "singles" and "doubles".The game developed in British India from the earlier game of battledore and shuttlecock.

HOCKEY

Hockey is a term used to denote a family of various types of both summer and winter team sports which originated on either an outdoor field, sheet of ice, or dry floor such as in a gymnasium. While these sports vary in specific rules, numbers of players, apparel, and playing surface.

BASKETBALL

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete with the primary objective of shooting a basketball (approximately 9.4 inches (24 cm) in diameter) through the defender's hoop (a basket 18 inches (46 cm) in diameter).

TENNIS

Tennis is a racket sport that is played either individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). Each player uses a tennis racket that is strung with cord to strike a hollow rubber ball covered with it felt over or around a net and into the opponent's court.

GOLF

Golf is a precision sport played on a vast outdoor course with the objective of completing it in the fewest strokes possible. It involves hitting a small ball into a series of holes with as few swings as possible. The game typically consists of 18 holes, but there are shorter versions with 9 holes.
